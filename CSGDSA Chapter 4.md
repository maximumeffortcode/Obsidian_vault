==Speeding Up Your Code With Big O==
==Bubble Sort==
==The Efficiency of Bubble Sort==
Contains 2 kinds of steps:
	1. Comparisons: Two numbers are compared with one another to determine which is greater
	2. Swaps: two numbers are swapped with one another in order to sort them

Has efficiency of 0(N^2), considered to be a relatively inefficient algorithm

0(N^2) also referred to as quadratic time
